# Hexagram 5 . WAITING (NOURISHMENT)

Hsu

Source: https://castiching.com/hexagrams/5-waiting-nourishment

## Intro

Hsu

Wilhelm/Baynes Translation

## The Judgement

WAITING. If you are sincere, You have light and success. Perseverance brings good fortune. It furthers one to cross the great water.

## The Image

Clouds rise up to the heavens: The image of WAITING. Thus the superior man eats and drinks, is joyous and of good cheer.

### Six at the top

One falls into the pit. Three uninvited guests arrive. Honor them, and in the end there will be good fortune.

The Image says: 'Uninvited guests arrive. Honor them, and in the end there will be good fortune.' Though the place is not the right one, one has not made a great mistake.

### Nine in the fifth place

Waiting at meat and drink. Perseverance brings good fortune.

The Image says: 'At meat and drink, perseverance brings good fortune.' This is due to the central and correct position.

### Six in the fourth place

Waiting in blood. Get out of the pit.

The Image says: 'Waiting in blood.' One must be obedient and listen.

### Nine in the third place

Waiting in the mud brings the enemy near.

The Image says: 'Waiting in the mud.' Calamity is in the outer trigram. 'He himself invites the robbers.' If one is reverent and cautious, one will not be defeated.

### Nine in the second place

Waiting on the sand. There is some gossip. The end brings good fortune.

The Image says: 'Waiting on the sand.' One is at ease in the middle. Although there is some gossip, it ends in good fortune.

### Nine at the beginning

Waiting in the meadow. It furthers one to abide in what is constant. No blame.

The Image says: 'Waiting in the meadow.' One does not challenge the difficulty. 'It furthers one to abide in what is constant. No blame.' One has not lost the regular way.

## The Art of Patience and Nourishment

Waiting in the meadow, on the sand, in the mud, in pools of blood, at a banquet, and in the pit... waiting, and waiting again.

Hexagram 5 ( Hsu /Waiting) describes the essential phase of accumulating strength. After the initial struggle of creation ( Hexagram 3 ) and the phase of education ( Hexagram 4 ), we are capable, but the time is not yet right to act.

For entrepreneurs and creators, this offers a critical roadmap for the "growth phase":

- Do Not Rush : Even if your team is trained and professional, you must not advance rashly against danger.

Do Not Rush : Even if your team is trained and professional, you must not advance rashly against danger.

- Nourish the Spirit : The hexagram famously advises us to "wait with wine and food." This means being generous, relaxed, and open-minded. Use this time to build morale and inner strength.

Nourish the Spirit : The hexagram famously advises us to "wait with wine and food." This means being generous, relaxed, and open-minded. Use this time to build morale and inner strength.

- Use Softness : The strategy is to use softness to overcome hardness. By holding to the center and maintaining correctness, you avoid exhaustion.

Use Softness : The strategy is to use softness to overcome hardness. By holding to the center and maintaining correctness, you avoid exhaustion.

Summary : Waiting is not passive; it is active preparation. It is about knowing that you are in a time of accumulation. By remaining calm and cheerful in the face of unpredictable changes, you ensure a harmonious and auspicious result.
